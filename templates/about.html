About NESW Puzzles

Welcome to NESW Puzzles! We created a bunch of puzzles inspired by scavenger hunts, escape rooms, and brain teasers!


The clues are hidden within various locations across the world, so we have provided a built-in Google Maps to help you search for them. Also remember that the Internet is your friend!


To start, sign in using the link at the top-right corner, or enter your name in the box in the top-right corner if you are already signed in.
